PM SETU Scheme

Modernizing ITIs nationwide through advanced infrastructure, industry collaboration, and future-ready skill development.

Scheme Details

Objectives

The PM SETU scheme aims to transform Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) into centers of excellence for skill development.

  • Upgrade infrastructure of ITIs with modern equipment
  • Enhance industry-institute collaboration
  • Introduce new age trades and technologies
  • Improve employability of ITI graduates
  • Establish ITIs as hubs for entrepreneurship

Key Features

The scheme introduces several innovative features to revolutionize vocational training in India.

  • Digital infrastructure and smart classrooms
  • Industry 4.0 skill development labs
  • Apprenticeship embedded training programs
  • International collaboration for best practices
  • Placement linked training modules
  • Green and sustainable technologies focus

Beneficiaries

PM SETU benefits multiple stakeholders in the skill development ecosystem.

  • ITI students across all states and union territories
  • ITI instructors and administrative staff
  • Industry partners seeking skilled workforce
  • MSMEs requiring trained technical personnel
  • State governments and skill development departments
  • Entrepreneurs in the vocational education space

Implementation Strategy

The scheme follows a phased implementation approach with clear monitoring mechanisms.

  • Phased implementation over 5 years
  • Centralized portal for scheme monitoring
  • State-level implementation committees
  • Regular third-party evaluations
  • Feedback mechanism from all stakeholders

Industry Partnerships

Strategic partnerships with industry leaders form a core component of the scheme.

  • MoUs with leading industrial organizations
  • Center of Excellence with industry collaboration
  • Guest lectures and workshops by industry experts
  • Industry-sponsored labs and equipment
  • On-the-job training opportunities

Training & Curriculum

Modern curriculum aligned with industry requirements and future technologies.

  • NSQF aligned curriculum framework
  • Focus on emerging technologies (AI, IoT, Robotics)
  • Soft skills and entrepreneurship training
  • Digital literacy and e-learning modules
  • International certification opportunities

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the full form of PM SETU?

PM SETU stands for "Pradhan Mantri Skill Enhancement and Training Upgrade" scheme. It is a flagship initiative aimed at modernizing Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) across India.

Who is eligible to benefit from the PM SETU scheme?

All Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) recognized by NCVT are eligible for upgradation under this scheme. Additionally, students enrolled in these ITIs, instructors, and industry partners collaborating with ITIs are key beneficiaries.

How can an ITI apply for PM SETU benefits?

ITIs can apply through the official PM SETU portal. The application process involves submitting institutional details, current infrastructure status, proposed upgradation plan, and necessary documentation as specified in the scheme guidelines.

What types of infrastructure upgrades are covered?

The scheme covers upgrades to workshops, laboratories, smart classrooms, digital infrastructure, library resources, hostel facilities (where applicable), and installation of modern equipment aligned with industry requirements.

Is there any financial assistance for students?

While the primary focus is on institutional upgradation, the scheme facilitates better placement opportunities and industry exposure for students. Some components may include stipends for apprenticeships and special industry-sponsored scholarships.

How does PM SETU promote industry collaboration?

The scheme mandates and facilitates MoUs between ITIs and industry partners. This includes guest lectures, curriculum input, equipment sponsorship, on-the-job training, apprenticeship opportunities, and placement support.
